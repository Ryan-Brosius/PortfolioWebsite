Wings of Harmony

2D Musical Game
Created in Unity using C#

Itch.io

GDD

This is a 2D musical rhythm-like game that I worked on with 3 other artists. I was the lead programmer of the project and it was my job to understand and program the team vision. Our idea was to create a rhythm game that helped teach and play songs. To do this, you play as a bird flying through the sky and you collect the correct note to play a tune. Once you collect all the notes for a level you play the tune at the end. And if you correctly collected all of the notes you proceded to the next level.
