Gun Jump

2D Platformer
First game I worked on with a team!
Developed in Unity using C#
Gun Jump was the first game I made with a group during my freshman year of college. My lead role was a developer where I learned to develop the movement and interaction of the game environment.

This was the first time I programmed for a game throughout a long period of time. And allthough it has its flaws, I like to keep this game a reminder of how far I have come as a programmer & game developer.

Unfortunately the repo no longer exists as my college took it down, but I do have a demo and a few photos below.
